About Kadion

Experienced, highly qualified Educational Technology leader with over 20 years of experience in K-12 with various roles in the classroom, in building, and in district administration. Effective leader and communicator, and works collaboratively with stakeholders at all levels to create a positive and inclusive learning environment. Passionate about using technology to improve student outcomes, and believes that all students have the potential to learn, regardless of their circumstances. Proven track record of success in leading and managing technology departments, developing and implementing technology initiatives, and providing leadership and support to educational staff. Oversaw a complete transformation of the Oxford Public Schools technology infrastructure, securing funding, implementing new systems, and leading the district to create a 1:1 device program Proficient in a wide range of IT disciplines including database management, networking, security, telecommunications, custom code, and query writing, and has a deep understanding of educational technology best practices.
