About Kadion

Hi, I’m Kadion Phillips.

If you’ve spent time at our local sports fields or tuned into a town committee meeting, we may have already met. I’m running for the Sturbridge Select Board for the local election on April 13, 2026, because I believe that deep roots and active service are the best foundations for leadership.

Why I’m Running

Our town is a special place, but keeping it that way requires more than just good intentions—it requires work, transparency, and a clear vision. I’ve spent years "in the trenches" of our local government and youth programs. I’m not just asking for your vote; I’m asking for the opportunity to continue the work I’m already doing for you.

My Vision for Sturbridge


Fiscal Responsibility: I believe in a government that respects your hard-earned money. I am committed to keeping taxes low by scrutinizing every dollar spent. We must prioritize essential services while exploring innovative solutions to maintain financial stability.

A Vibrant Community: From our classrooms to our hiking trails, I want to make Sturbridge a place where families thrive and visitors feel welcome. This means supporting our schools, expanding recreational spaces, and preserving the unique historic character that makes us "Sturbridge."

Smart Economic Growth: To keep our tax base strong, we must be a partner to our local businesses. By streamlining processes and promoting Sturbridge as a prime location for commerce, we can strengthen our tax base and create new opportunities for our residents.

Experience You Can Count On

Leadership is about collaboration. Whether I’m serving as Vice Chair of the Community Preservation Committee, working on the Recreation and Cable Advisory Committees, or coaching Sturbridge Youth Basketball, Soccer, and Little League, my goal has always been the same: to listen, to solve problems, and to move us forward. Professionally, my work with the Massachusetts Educational Technology Administrators Association has given me the tools to manage complex systems and maintain high standards of accountability. I am ready to bring that same professional rigor to the Select Board.

Join Me on April 13th

A prosperous future for Sturbridge starts with a government that works for all of us. I would be honored to have your support and your vote.

Let’s keep Sturbridge moving forward—together.
